Visitation

A fit parent is usually entitled to visitation with his or her child. The amount, time, location and nature of the visitation will be determined by the Family Court based on a number of factors, including what is in best interest of the child.

For case law discussing visitation, see McGregor v. McGregor, 255 S.C. 179, 177 S.E.2d 599 (1970).
